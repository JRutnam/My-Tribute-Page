Trivium, who they are and why I love their music

  • Trivium are a North American heavy metal band, that first formed in Orlando Florida in 1999.
  • Current Frontman Matt Heafy was first brought in to the band as a lead guitarist in 1999, at the age of only 13.
  • Arguably the most recognisable line up of Trivium was from 2004 - 2010, the only difference from their current line up being founding member Travis Smith on the drums
  • Long time followers and fans of Trivium will be well aware of the bands evolving musical style. Something that can be attributed to their ongoing success on the world stage.
    • Personally I believe the 2015 album Until the world goes cold and the 2017 album The sin and the sentence truely capture the essence of Trivium and demonstrate why they have continued to be successful at creating a universally pleasing sound

5 songs I would recommend to any new listener

  • Pull harder on the strings of your martyr
  • Shogun
  • In Waves
  • Down from the Sky
  • The Heart from your hate

Overall as a heavy metal fan, or simply a fan of well composed and well written songs. In my opinion Trivium have something to offer every music listener and I would highly recommend listening to them
